Why the name should stay aiButler

`aiButler` is the strongest master brand because it says exactly what the product does while leaving room for voice clone, avatar, CRM, and specialist agent workflows. `Butler Clone` is too narrow. `Mira` is better used as a specialist sales agent inside the Butler system, not as the public brand for the whole product.

Master brand

`aiButler` is the product. It should own the public narrative, the domain, and the repository identity.

Specialist agent

`Mira` can continue as the sales and outreach employee that lives inside Butler rather than competing with it.

Feature layer

The clone, avatar, and voice likeness are premium capabilities inside aiButler, not the product category itself.

What aiButler is becoming

A phone-first AI companion that behaves like a personal operator: it remembers context, keeps relationships warm, protects your time, and can safely act through a trusted local runtime.

Phone-native companion

Your phone is the daily Butler surface for briefings, approvals, relationship memory, follow-up planning, and quick actions.

Trusted local operator

Your desktop handles secrets, files, supervised computer use, and high-trust execution through approvals and receipts.

Recommended brand architecture

Keep the naming stack simple so users, contributors, and future customers understand the system immediately.

Public naming

  • Product: aiButler
  • Domain: aiButler.me
  • Repo: aibutler
  • Tagline: The AI butler who can manage your life.

Internal product layers

  • Butler Mobile
  • Butler Desktop
  • Butler Runtime
  • Mira for sales and outreach

What to avoid

  • Do not lead with “clone” as the category
  • Do not make Mira the umbrella brand
  • Do not position this as just another assistant chat app
