# aiButler Product Profile

## Naming decision

The public product name should be `aiButler`.

That is the strongest choice because:

- you own `aiButler.me`
- it immediately communicates the role of the product
- it is broader than “clone” and more durable than a temporary codename
- it leaves room for voice clone, avatar, CRM, and operator workflows inside the same brand

`Mira` should not be the primary public brand for this fork. It can live on as:

- a lineage note
- a specialist agent inside Butler
- an internal or feature-level name for sales / outreach workflows

`Butler Clone` is not the best primary name. It narrows the product too early and makes the whole thing sound like a voice-copy gimmick. The clone/avatar should be a capability inside aiButler, not the brand itself.

## Core product

`aiButler` is the AI butler who can manage your life.

More precisely:

aiButler is a phone-first AI companion that remembers people, protects your time, manages relationship context, and can safely act across your digital world through a trusted local runtime.

## Category

aiButler sits at the intersection of:

- personal AI assistant
- phone-based CRM companion
- executive operator
- supervised desktop agent

It should not be described as “just a chatbot” or “just an AI clone.”

## One-line value proposition

aiButler is your phone-first AI butler: a personal chief of staff with a CRM brain that helps manage your life, your relationships, and your next moves.

## Short pitch

aiButler keeps track of who matters, what needs attention, and what should happen next. It lives on your phone for quick context, planning, and approvals, and reaches your desktop when it is time to execute higher-trust work.

## What makes it different

- `Phone first`
  It starts where people actually live: their phone.
- `CRM for real life`
  It remembers contacts, commitments, follow-ups, and momentum.
- `Butler, not chatbot`
  It is there to manage, organize, and act, not just answer questions.
- `Approval first`
  It does not silently take over your world.
- `Local-first trust`
  Sensitive actions route through a trusted local runtime and system keychains.

## Messaging hierarchy

### Brand

`aiButler`

### Product description

Phone-first AI butler and CRM companion.

### Capability layers

- `Butler Mobile`
  The phone companion for context, quick actions, and approvals.
- `Butler Desktop`
  The trusted local operator surface.
- `Butler Runtime`
  The shared policy, memory, tool, and receipt engine.
- `Mira`
  A specialist sales / outreach employee inside Butler, not the master brand.

## Tagline options

Recommended primary tagline:

`The AI butler who can manage your life.`

Strong alternates:

- `Your phone-first AI chief of staff.`
- `The CRM brain for your life and work.`
- `Your AI butler for people, priorities, and next moves.`

## Who it is for

Primary:

- founders
- operators
- creators
- consultants
- small teams

Secondary:

- ambitious professionals who are drowning in coordination drag
- people who need a relationship-memory layer more than another chat app

## What the first public version should promise

Promise only what the product can soon demonstrate:

- daily executive briefings
- relationship and follow-up memory
- secure phone-to-desktop pairing
- approved local execution
- voice-native interaction

Do not lead with grand future abstractions when the strongest story is already visible:

`aiButler helps you keep your life, relationships, and execution layer organized from your phone.`

## What not to call it

Avoid these as primary names:

- `Butler Clone`
- `Mira Clone`
- `Jarvis for X`
- `AI Twin` as the master brand

These can be feature concepts, but they weaken the clarity of the product category.

## Public naming recommendation

Use this naming stack:

- Product: `aiButler`
- Domain: `aiButler.me`
- Repo: `aibutler`
- Runtime: `Butler Runtime`
- Desktop app: `Butler Desktop`
- Mobile app: `Butler Mobile`
- Sales/outreach specialist: `Mira`

That gives you one clean brand with room to grow.
